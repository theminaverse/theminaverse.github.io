# About Me

Hi. I'm an Economics master's student, which is a fancy way of saying I spend my days:
- Reading papers I don't fully understand ✨
- Questioning whether the economy actually works
- Wondering if my thesis matters in a world held together by memes and hope
- Drinking coffee like it's water


I created this blog to document my spiral into understanding economics, getting through graduate school and the existential dread that comes with it. It's part journal, part research repository, part cry for help.



If you're here thinking I have it all figured out, you're in the wrong place. I'm here learning, breaking things, and occasionally discovering something cool. 

Welcome to the chaos.
